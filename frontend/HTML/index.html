About Us

Green Belt

With Green Belt EHR, healthcare providers can securely access patient information, manage appointments, and track medical history with ease. Our Service

Patient File Management

Efficient management of patient files is crucial in healthcare settings. Our system provides a streamlined approach to organizing, storing, and accessing patient records. This ensures that healthcare providers can quickly retrieve vital information, leading to better patient care and more accurate diagnoses.

Labaratory

Our laboratory services are equipped with state-of-the-art technology to perform a wide range of diagnostic tests. From routine blood work to specialized genetic testing, we provide accurate and timely results. Our skilled technicians and advanced equipment ensure that every test is conducted with precision, helping healthcare providers make informed decisions about patient treatment plans.

Pharmacy

We maintain a comprehensive inventory of pharmaceuticals and utilize a sophisticated system to manage prescriptions and medication dispensing. This minimizes the risk of errors and ensures that patients have access to the medications they need for their health and well-being.

x-ray

Our X-ray services offer advanced imaging solutions to assist in the diagnosis and treatment of various medical conditions. Utilizing cutting-edge technology, we provide clear and detailed images that help healthcare providers identify issues such as fractures, infections, and tumors.

Billing

We provide transparent and accurate billing processes, ensuring that patients understand their charges and insurance coverage. Our system helps reduce administrative burden, allowing healthcare providers to focus more on patient care.
